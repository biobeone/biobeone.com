Message from the Founder

 

Thank you for visiting the BioBeOne website.

These days, even for large enterprises it takes longer than expected to develop and provide innovative drugs and regenerative medical products for patients.
One way of overcoming this problem is through collaboration with suitable business partners.

Our mission is to help you find the best business partners in Japan and all over the world to ensure the steady progress of research and development through collaboration. We also aim to expedite the delivery of these products to patients who are desperately seeking treatment. To assist in these objectives we founded BioBeOne Inc.

Please join us and play a role in the global bridge to promote partnering and open innovation.

Yukio Kitai
Founder, President and CEO
